About Me

Sports Photography

My sports photography journey began back in January 2021.

After a major hip injury resulted in reconstructive surgery, I was told I was going to have to step away from the track for at least six months. It was during this time when i first began my photogrpahy career and have been working hard loving every moment since!

In January of 2023 I shifted my work towards freelance options and have since began working for various sports teams at a varity of Division I schools.

The schools I have freelanced for are as followed:

  1. South Carolina, Baseball
  2. Mississippi State, Track and Field
  3. Georgia, Equestrian
  4. Miami, Baseball
  5. Monmouth, Track and Field
  6. Furman, Women's Lacrosse
  7. Toledo, Men's Basketball
